# Competitor hands-on study (group-social / meet-IRL apps)

Not dating apps. Our app = spontaneous "meet nearby people right now to do an activity"
(group-friend, like Meetup / Flock). Bumble/Hinge excluded (dating).

Studied hands-on on the Pixel (real taps, not just screenshots): install → register →
**actually join something + see what happens + actually walk the create-activity flow +
poke every usable function**. Filled per app only after doing it for real.

Per-app template:

- **Model** — group vs 1:1, scheduled vs right-now, activity vs chat.
- **Join flow** — how you join, is it gated/paid, what happens AFTER you join (chat? confirm? see members?).
- **Create flow** — how you start an activity, steps, gating, cost.
- **Nearby / now** — does it do proximity + "right now", or future/scheduled?
- **Monetization** — free? organizer fee? subscription?
- **Safety / gender** — verification, women-only, report/block.
- **vs us** — what they do better, and the gap we fill.

Android-installable: Meetup, Flock, HeyMandi, Timeleft, Pie (+ Nextdoor, light).
iOS-only (research separately): Mozi (Ev Williams, Android waitlisted), 222.

## Meetup ✅ hands-on (logged in via Google, HK region)

- **Model** — scheduled FUTURE group events (tabs: Today / Tomorrow / Weekend / All). Every event is run by a **Group** (e.g. "Friend to Friend hangout", 4.81★, 186 reviews). Category-driven (Social / Hobbies / Sports / Travel). Map view exists.
- **Join** — tap **Attend** → often a **registration form with organizer questions** ("what's your name? leave your WhatsApp for the event") → **Complete registration**. So joining = friction + the organizer harvests your contact (WhatsApp). Free events are still gated behind this. Big events show "1366 going".
- **Create** — **NO create entry for a normal user.** To run events you must start/own a Group = **Meetup Organizer subscription (paid, ~US$16–25/mo)**. Very high barrier to post anything.
- **Nearby / now** — shows distance (10.5 km, 4.6 km) but everything is **future-scheduled**, never "right now". Location = filter, not the point.
- **Monetization** — organizer subscription. Attendees free.
- **Safety** — real-name-ish via Google, reviews on groups; no women-only.
- **vs us** — Meetup is the **organized / scheduled / paid-to-post / group-centric** end. Our gap: **spontaneous, instant, free to post, no group needed, right-now**. Their "join" hands your WhatsApp to a stranger org; ours keeps it in-app.
